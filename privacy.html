Afterglow

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2025

1. Overview

Afterglow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal mood tracking tool that allows users to log daily moods, grades, and journal notes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Afterglow, we may collect the following:

  • Email address — collected when you sign in with Google, used solely to identify your account
  • Mood entries — the grade (A+ to F), date, and any written notes you log
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics events collected via Mixpanel to help us improve the product

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or any other personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and sync your mood tracking entries across devices
  • Display your personal statistics and charts
  • Improve the app experience through anonymous usage analytics

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your mood data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Guest users' data is stored locally on their device only and is never sent to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Afterglow uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — for secure data storage. Governed by Supabase's Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel — for anonymous usage analytics. Governed by Mixpanel's Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

8. Data Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all associated mood data, email us at barbaraposadamen@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account". We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Afterglow is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at barbaraposadamen@gmail.com
